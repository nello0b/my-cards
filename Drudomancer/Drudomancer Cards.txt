DRUDOMANCER CARD INFORMATION
============================

Source: Saved DuelingBook deck page (deck ID 15995977)

Archetype overview
------------------
Drudomancer is an Illusion-based archetype focused on revealing monsters in the
hand, gaining additional Normal Summons, Tribute Summoning its stronger monsters,
and recycling its Spell/Trap Cards. As is common for Illusion monsters, its
monsters generally cannot destroy or be destroyed by the monsters they battle.


1. Drudomancer Soulwisp
-----------------------
Card Type: Effect Monster
Attribute: LIGHT
Level: 2
Monster Type: Illusion / Effect
ATK: 800
DEF: 1000

If you control no Special Summoned monsters, you can Special Summon this card
(from your hand). If this card battles a monster, neither can be destroyed by
that battle. If this card is Normal or Special Summoned: You can add 1
"Drudomancer" Spell/Trap from your Deck to your hand, also you cannot Special
Summon monsters for the rest of this turn, except Illusion monsters. You can
only use this effect of "Drudomancer Soulwisp" once per turn.


2. Drudomancer Soulguide
------------------------
Card Type: Effect Monster
Attribute: DARK
Level: 3
Monster Type: Illusion / Effect
ATK: 1500
DEF: 1000

If you control no Special Summoned monsters, you can Special Summon this card
(from your hand). If this card battles a monster, neither can be destroyed by
that battle. If this card is Tributed: You can target 1 other "Drudomancer"
card in your GY or banishment; add both it and this card to your hand. You can
only use this effect of "Drudomancer Soulguide" once per turn.


3. Drudomancer Soulcharmer
--------------------------
Card Type: Effect Monster
Attribute: LIGHT
Level: 4
Monster Type: Illusion / Effect
ATK: 1800
DEF: 1000

If you control no Special Summoned monsters, you can Special Summon this card
(from your hand). If this card battles a monster, neither can be destroyed by
that battle. You can banish this card from your field or GY; Special Summon up
to 2 "Drudomancer Tokens" (Illusion/LIGHT/Level 1/ATK 0/DEF 1000), also you
cannot Special Summon monsters for the rest of this turn, except Illusion
monsters. You can only use this effect of "Drudomancer Soulcharmer" once per
turn.


4. Drudomancer Soultamer
------------------------
Card Type: Effect Monster
Attribute: DARK
Level: 5
Monster Type: Illusion / Effect
ATK: 2400
DEF: 1000

If this card battles a monster, neither can be destroyed by that battle. You
can reveal this card in your hand, and keep it revealed for the rest of this
turn; discard 1 card, and if you do, add 1 "Drudomancer" monster from your Deck
to your hand, except "Drudomancer Soultamer". You can only use this effect of
"Drudomancer Soultamer" once per turn. If this card is Tribute Summoned: You
can Set 1 "Drudomancer" Spell/Trap from your Deck, then you can destroy 1
Spell/Trap on the field.


5. Drudomancer Soulhunter
-------------------------
Card Type: Effect Monster
Attribute: LIGHT
Level: 6
Monster Type: Illusion / Effect
ATK: 2500
DEF: 1000

If this card battles a monster, neither can be destroyed by that battle.
During the Main Phase, if you control a "Drudomancer" monster (Quick Effect):
You can reveal this card in your hand, and keep it revealed for the rest of
this turn; immediately after this effect resolves, Normal Summon 1 Illusion
monster. You can only use this effect of "Drudomancer Soulhunter" once per
turn. If this card is Tribute Summoned: You can target 1 card on the field;
send it to the GY, then you can banish 1 card from either GY.


6. Disdrudomancer Soulstealer
-----------------------------
Card Type: Effect Monster
Attribute: DARK
Level: 7
Monster Type: Illusion / Effect
ATK: 2700
DEF: 1000

If this card battles a monster, neither can be destroyed by that battle. You
can reveal this card in your hand, and keep it revealed for the rest of this
turn; Special Summon 1 monster from either GY in face-down Defense Position,
but destroy it during the End Phase. You can only use this effect of
"Disdrudomancer Soulstealer" once per turn. If this card is Tribute Summoned:
You can target up to 2 cards in either GY; banish them, then you can send that
many "Drudomancer" cards from your Deck to the GY.


7. Drudomancer Soulspinner
--------------------------
Card Type: Effect Monster
Attribute: LIGHT
Level: 8
Monster Type: Illusion / Effect
ATK: 2800
DEF: 1000

If this card battles a monster, neither can be destroyed by that battle. You
can reveal this card in your hand, and keep it revealed for the rest of this
turn; discard 1 card, and if you do, Special Summon 1 "Drudomancer" monster
from your Deck, except "Drudomancer Soulspinner". You can only use this effect
of "Drudomancer Soulspinner" once per turn. If this card is Tribute Summoned:
You can either destroy all monsters your opponent controls or return them to
the hand.


8. Drudomancer Spirit Journey
-----------------------------
Card Type: Continuous Spell

During your Main Phase: You can add 1 "Drudomancer" monster from your Deck to
your hand. You can only use this effect of "Drudomancer Spirit Journey" once
per turn. Once per turn, if you would Tribute a monster for the Tribute Summon
of a revealed "Drudomancer" monster, you can Tribute 1 monster your opponent
controls even though you do not control it. At the end of the Damage Step, if
an Illusion monster you control battled a monster: You can return both monsters
to the hand.


9. Drudomancer Eulogy
---------------------
Card Type: Quick-Play Spell

Target 1 Spell/Trap your opponent controls, or, if a "Drudomancer" monster is
revealed in your hand, you can target 1 monster your opponent controls instead;
destroy it. If this card is in your GY: You can target 3 Illusion monsters in
your GY and/or banishment; Set this card (but banish it when it leaves the
field), and if you do, shuffle those targets into the Deck. You can only use
each effect of "Drudomancer Eulogy" once per turn.


10. Envy of Drudomancer
-----------------------
Card Type: Quick-Play Spell

Target 1 face-up Special Summoned monster your opponent controls; negate its
effects (until the end of this turn), also if a "Drudomancer" monster is
revealed in your hand, you can take control of that monster until the End
Phase. If this card is in your GY: You can target 1 Level 5 or higher Illusion
monster you control; Set this card (but banish it when it leaves the field),
and if you do, return that monster to the hand. You can only use each effect of
"Envy of Drudomancer" once per turn.


11. Drudomancer Respite
-----------------------
Card Type: Counter Trap

When a Spell/Trap Card is activated, while you control a Level 5 or higher
Illusion monster: Negate the activation, and if you do, destroy it. If a
"Drudomancer" monster is revealed in your hand, you can activate this card
from your hand. If this card is in your GY: You can Set this card (but banish
it when it leaves the field), and if you do, immediately after this effect
resolves, Normal Summon 1 Illusion monster. You can only use 1 "Drudomancer
Respite" effect per turn, and only once that turn.


12. Drudomancer Soul Echo
-------------------------
Card Type: Continuous Trap

During the Main Phase, you can: Immediately after this effect resolves, Normal
Summon 1 Illusion monster. You can only use this effect of "Drudomancer Soul
Echo" once per turn. If your Illusion monster attacks a Defense Position
monster, inflict piercing battle damage to your opponent. Once per turn,
during the End Phase: Return 1 Level 5 or higher Illusion monster you control
to the hand or destroy this card. If a "Drudomancer" monster is revealed in
your hand, you can activate this card from your hand.
